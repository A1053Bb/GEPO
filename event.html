University Launches New Robotics Lab



Our college has officially opened a state-of-the-art Robotics Lab equipped
with the latest technology for research and experimentation. This facility
will provide students and faculty with the resources to explore advancements
in robotics, automation, and artificial intelligence. The lab will host
workshops, guest lectures, and hands-on projects to enhance learning and
innovation.

Student Team Wins International Hackathon



A team of our students has triumphed at the Global Tech Hackathon held in
San Francisco, winning first place for their innovative app that promotes
energy conservation. The app uses smart technology to help users monitor and reduce
their energy consumption in real-time. This victory showcases the talent and creativity
of our students in applying technology for sustainable solutions.

New Scholarship Program Announced for STEM Students



Our college has launched a new scholarship program aimed at supporting
underrepresented students pursuing degrees in STEM fields.
The "Innovators Scholarship" will provide financial assistance as well as
mentorship opportunities with industry leaders. This initiative aims to
encourage diversity and inclusion within technical disciplines.

Annual Technical Symposium Scheduled for March



The college is excited to announce its Annual Technical Symposium,
scheduled for March 5-7, 2025. This year's theme is "Innovating for a
Sustainable Future," featuring expert speakers, panel discussions, and
student presentations on cutting-edge technologies in
environmental engineering, renewable energy, and sustainable design.
Participants will have the opportunity to network with professionals and
showcase their research.
